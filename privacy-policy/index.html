Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 18, 2026

Welcome to ZodiacMind. Your privacy is our priority. This policy explains how we handle your information within the ZodiacMind Android application.

Core Principle: ZodiacMind is designed to be a "Privacy-First" app. We do not send your personal birth data to any external servers. All astrological calculations and PDF reports are generated locally on your device.

1. Information We Collect (On-Device Only)

To generate accurate horoscopes and matching reports, the app asks for:

  • Personal Details: Name, Gender.
  • Birth Details: Date of Birth, Time of Birth, and Place of Birth (Location).

This data is used only to perform astronomical calculations within the app. Once a PDF report is generated, the data remains on your phone unless you choose to share the PDF via other apps.

2. Third-Party Services

While we do not store your data, we use industry-standard third-party tools to improve the app experience and support development:

A. Google Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase to understand how users interact with the app (e.g., which features are used most). This data is anonymous and does not include your name or birth details.

B. Google AdMob

Our app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may use pseudonymous identifiers (like your Advertising ID) to show relevant ads. You can manage these settings in your Android device's Google Settings.

3. PDF Generation & Storage

The app requires permission to save the generated Horoscope PDF to your device's storage. You have full control over these files and can delete them at any time.

4. Data Security

Since your data never leaves your device to reach our servers, the security of your birth information is protected by your own device's security measures.

5. Children’s Privacy

ZodiacMind does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has provided us with information, please contact us.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
