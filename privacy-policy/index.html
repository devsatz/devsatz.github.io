Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

ZodiacMind ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles user information.

No Account Required

ZodiacMind does not require you to create an account or sign in. You can use all features of the app without providing any account-related information.

Information You Provide

The app may require you to enter personal details such as your name, date of birth, time of birth, and birth location.

These details are used solely for generating horoscope charts, compatibility analysis, and PDF reports.

Data Storage and Usage

We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to any external servers.

All calculations, including horoscope generation and compatibility matching, are performed locally on your device.

Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with the app.

This includes general usage information such as feature usage frequency and app engagement. This data is anonymized and does not include personally identifiable information.

Advertising (Future Use)

ZodiacMind may use Google AdMob in the future to display advertisements.

AdMob may collect and use certain data, such as device information and advertising identifiers, to provide personalized or non-personalized ads.

Users will be able to manage ad preferences through their device settings.

Notifications

The app may request permission to send notifications.

Notifications are used to engage users, such as reminders to check the app or important updates. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

Children’s Privacy

ZodiacMind is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since all data is processed locally on the device and not transmitted to our servers, we do not store any personal data of users, including children.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can guide you appropriately.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Firebase Analytics and may use Google AdMob, services provided by Google, to improve user experience and app performance.

Data Security

Since all personal data remains on your device and is not transmitted externally, your information stays under your control.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
