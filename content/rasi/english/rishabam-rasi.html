Rishabam (Taurus)

Rishabam Rasi, represented by the Bull and known as Taurus in Western astrology, is the second sign of the zodiac. Ruled by the planet Venus (Sukran), this rasi embodies stability, beauty, and a love for the finer things in life. It is an Earth sign, making its natives practical and well-grounded.

Personality

People born in Rishabam Rasi are known for their calm and composed demeanor. They are highly reliable and value security above all else. A Rishabam native is often the "anchor" in their social circle, providing a sense of stability to those around them. They have a deep appreciation for art, music, and nature. While they are usually gentle, they can be incredibly firm when their values are challenged. They prefer a steady pace of life and rarely rush into decisions.

Strengths

The greatest strength of a Rishabam person is their persistence. Once they commit to a task, they will see it through to the end, regardless of the obstacles. They are incredibly loyal friends and partners. Their practical nature helps them manage resources and finances very well. They possess immense patience and a strong sense of responsibility. Their artistic inclination often makes them creative and tasteful in everything they do.

Weaknesses

Resistance to change is a significant weakness for Rishabam Rasi. They are creatures of habit and can become very uncomfortable when forced out of their routine. This can manifest as extreme stubbornness. At times, their love for comfort can turn into laziness or over-indulgence. They can also be quite possessive of their belongings and relationships, leading to friction with people who value more freedom.

Career

Rishabam natives excel in professions that require patience, stability, and an eye for detail. They are naturally gifted in the fields of finance, banking, and real estate. Because Venus rules them, they also find great success in creative industries like interior design, fashion, music, and jewelry. Their connection to the Earth sign also makes them good at architecture, agriculture, and hospitality. They prefer stable jobs with clear growth paths over risky ventures.

Marriage

In marriage, Rishabam individuals are devoted and romantic. They look for a stable and long-term commitment. They enjoy creating a comfortable and beautiful home for their family. They are most compatible with other Earth signs like Kanni (Virgo) and Magaram (Capricorn), who share their practical outlook. They also share a good bond with Kadagam (Cancer) and Meenam (Pisces) due to their emotional depth and nurturing nature.

Lucky Color

The lucky colors for Rishabam are White, Cream, and Pink. These colors resonate with the soft and elegant energy of Venus.

Lucky Number

The lucky numbers for Rishabam natives are 2, 7, and 9. These numbers are believed to bring harmony and progress into their lives.

Lucky Stone

Diamond (Heera) is the primary lucky stone for Rishabam. White Sapphire or Zircon are also often recommended to strengthen the influence of Venus and bring prosperity.
