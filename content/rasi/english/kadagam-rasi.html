Kadagam (Cancer)

Kadagam Rasi, known as Cancer in Western astrology, is the fourth sign of the zodiac. It is represented by the Crab and is ruled by the Moon (Chandran). As a Water sign, Kadagam natives are deeply emotional, intuitive, and nurturing, with a strong connection to home and family.

Personality

Individuals born in Kadagam Rasi are known for their sensitive and caring nature. They possess a high degree of emotional intelligence and can easily sense the feelings of others. Like the crab's shell, they can be protective and sometimes retreat into their own space when feeling vulnerable. They are deeply loyal to their loved ones and will go to great lengths to ensure their safety and happiness. Their moods can sometimes fluctuate with the phases of the moon.

Strengths

The primary strength of Kadagam is their immense empathy and nurturing spirit. They are excellent caregivers and loyal friends. Their intuition is a powerful guide, often helping them navigate complex situations. They are highly imaginative and creative, with a strong appreciation for tradition and history. Their tenacity and protective nature make them the "motherly" figure in many social and professional circles.

Weaknesses

Their deep sensitivity can sometimes lead to moodiness or over-emotional reactions. Kadagam natives can be prone to dwelling on the past, making it difficult for them to move on from emotional hurts. They can be quite cautious and sometimes suspicious of newcomers until trust is established. At times, their protective nature can border on being possessive or clingy in relationships.

Career

Kadagam individuals thrive in careers that allow them to care for others or use their creative talents. They make excellent doctors, nurses, counselors, and teachers. Their connection to the home often leads them to success in real estate, interior design, or the hospitality industry. Their intuitive nature also makes them good at business and finance. They prefer a work environment that feels supportive and "family-like."

Marriage

In marriage, Kadagam Rasi people are extremely devoted and seek emotional security. They value a stable home life and are very protective of their spouse and children. They are most compatible with Earth signs like Rishabam (Taurus) and Kanni (Virgo), who provide the stability they crave. They also share a deep emotional bond with fellow Water signs like Viruchigam (Scorpio) and Meenam (Pisces).

Lucky Color

The lucky colors for Kadagam Rasi are White, Silver, and Cream. These colors reflect the soothing and pure energy of the Moon.

Lucky Number

The lucky numbers for Kadagam natives are 2, 7, and 11. These numbers are believed to bring emotional balance and prosperity.

Lucky Stone

Pearl (Moti) is the recommended lucky stone for Kadagam. Wearing a pearl can help calm their emotions, improve mental peace, and strengthen their intuition.
