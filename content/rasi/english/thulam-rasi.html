Thulam (Libra)

Thulam Rasi, known as Libra in Western astrology, is the seventh sign of the zodiac. It is represented by the Scales, symbolizing balance, justice, and harmony. Ruled by Venus (Sukran), the planet of love and beauty, Thulam is an Air sign. Its natives are known for their charm, diplomatic nature, and desire for peace.

Personality

Individuals born in Thulam Rasi are the peacemakers of the zodiac. They possess a natural charm that makes them very popular in social circles. They have a strong sense of justice and always try to see both sides of a situation. Their personality is characterized by elegance, politeness, and a refined taste. They dislike conflict and will go to great lengths to avoid it. They value partnerships and are often at their best when they are collaborating with others.

Strengths

The primary strength of Thulam is their diplomatic skill. They are experts at resolving conflicts and bringing people together. Their sense of fairness and balance makes them highly objective. They are also very sociable and possess excellent communication skills. Their appreciation for beauty and art often gives them a creative edge. Their kindness and willingness to help others make them cherished companions.

Weaknesses

The desire to please everyone can lead to indecisiveness, which is a significant weakness for Thulam natives. They often struggle to make choices because they weigh all options so carefully. Their aversion to conflict can sometimes lead to them being passive or avoiding necessary confrontations. At times, they may appear superficial or overly concerned with outward appearances. They may also find it difficult to say "no" to people.

Career

Thulam individuals excel in careers that require negotiation, mediation, and a sense of aesthetics. They make excellent lawyers, judges, diplomats, and human resources professionals. Their creative talents often lead them to success in interior design, fashion, photography, or art. Their social skills also make them very effective in public relations, sales, and counseling. They prefer a work environment that is harmonious and visually pleasing.

Marriage

In marriage, Thulam Rasi people are extremely romantic and committed. They view marriage as a true partnership and strive for balance and harmony in their relationship. They are most compatible with fellow Air signs like Mithunam (Gemini) and Kumbam (Aquarius), who share their intellectual interests. They also share a good bond with Simmam (Leo) and Dhanusu (Sagittarius) due to their shared love for social life and excitement.

Lucky Color

The lucky colors for Thulam are White, Light Blue, and Pink. These colors reflect the peaceful and harmonious energy of Venus.

Lucky Number

The lucky numbers for Thulam natives are 6, 15, and 24. Number 6 is particularly auspicious as it is the number associated with Venus.

Lucky Stone

Diamond (Heera) is the primary lucky stone for Thulam. White Sapphire or Opal are also recommended to enhance the positive influence of Venus and bring prosperity and love.
