Meenam (Pisces)

Meenam Rasi, known as Pisces in Western astrology, is the twelfth and final sign of the zodiac. It is represented by two Fish swimming in opposite directions and is ruled by Jupiter (Guru). As a Water sign, Meenam natives are deeply compassionate, imaginative, and spiritually inclined.

Personality

Individuals born in Meenam Rasi are the dreamers of the zodiac. They possess a sensitive and gentle personality. Their empathy is boundless, and they often feel the emotions of others as their own. Their personality is characterized by kindness, intuition, and a deep connection to the spiritual world. They are often artistic and find joy in creative pursuits. While they are very friendly, they also need time alone to recharge their emotional energy.

Strengths

The primary strength of Meenam is their immense compassion. They are the first to help someone in need and are excellent listeners. Their intuition is a powerful guide, often helping them navigate life's complexities with a sense of knowing. They are highly creative and possess a rich imagination. Their selfless nature and ability to forgive make them deeply loved by their friends and family. Their adaptability allows them to get along with diverse groups of people.

Weaknesses

On the downside, their deep sensitivity can sometimes make them overly emotional or prone to escapism. Meenam natives may find it difficult to face reality and may retreat into their dream world when things get tough. They can be very indecisive and may struggle with setting boundaries in their relationships. At times, their selfless nature can lead to them being taken advantage of by others. They may also struggle with mood swings due to their absorption of surrounding energy.

Career

Meenam individuals excel in careers that involve healing, creativity, and social service. They make excellent doctors, nurses, therapists, and counselors. Their artistic talents often lead them to success in music, painting, poetry, and filmmaking. Their connection to the water often draws them to marine-related fields. They also thrive in spiritual and philosophical roles where they can guide others. They prefer work that is meaningful and allows them to use their imagination.

Marriage

In marriage, Meenam Rasi people are extremely devoted and romantic. They seek a deep emotional and spiritual connection with their partner. They are most compatible with fellow Water signs like Kadagam (Cancer) and Viruchigam (Scorpio), who understand their emotional depth. They also share a good bond with Earth signs like Rishabam (Taurus) and Magaram (Capricorn), who provide the stability and grounding they need.

Lucky Color

The lucky colors for Meenam are Yellow, Cream, and Light Green. These colors represent the wisdom of Jupiter and the soothing energy of water.

Lucky Number

The lucky numbers for Meenam natives are 3, 7, and 9. Number 3 is particularly auspicious as it is associated with Jupiter.

Lucky Stone

Yellow Sapphire (Pukhraj) is the recommended lucky stone for Meenam. Wearing it can help enhance their wisdom, health, and spiritual growth.
