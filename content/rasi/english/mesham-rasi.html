Mesham (Aries)

Mesham, known as Aries in Western astrology, is the first sign of the zodiac. It represents the beginning of the astrological cycle and is ruled by the fiery planet Mars. People born under this rasi are known for their vibrant energy and pioneering spirit.

Personality

Individuals born in Mesham Rasi are natural-born leaders. They possess a magnetic personality and an infectious enthusiasm that inspires others. They are often the first to start new projects and aren't afraid to take risks. Their personality is characterized by honesty and directness; you will always know where you stand with a Mesham person. They value independence and prefer to carve their own path rather than following the crowd.

Strengths

The primary strength of Mesham is their courage. They face challenges head-on without hesitation. Their determination is unparalleled; once they set a goal, they pursue it with single-minded focus. They are also highly confident and optimistic, believing that they can overcome any obstacle. Their honesty makes them reliable friends and partners who will always tell you the truth.

Weaknesses

On the flip side, their fiery nature can lead to impulsiveness. Mesham individuals often act before they think, which can lead to unnecessary complications. They can be quite impatient, wanting results immediately. Their short temper is another common trait, although they tend to forgive and forget just as quickly as they got angry. At times, their strong-willed nature can come across as egoistic or stubborn.

Career

Mesham individuals thrive in careers that offer excitement and authority. They are excellent entrepreneurs, as they love the thrill of building something from scratch. Their competitive nature makes them great in sports and sales. Many Mesham natives also find success in the military, police force, or emergency services, where their quick thinking and bravery are highly valued. They prefer leadership roles over subordinate positions.

Marriage

In marriage and relationships, Mesham Rasi people are passionate and devoted. They seek a partner who can match their energy levels and respect their need for independence. They are most compatible with Simmam (Leo) and Dhanusu (Sagittarius), as these fire signs share their zest for life. A marriage with a Mesham individual is never dull, as they constantly seek new ways to keep the spark alive.

Lucky Color

The lucky color for Mesham Rasi is Red. This color represents energy, passion, and the power of their ruling planet, Mars.

Lucky Number

The lucky numbers for Mesham natives are 9, 18, and 27. Number 9 is particularly auspicious as it is associated with Mars.

Lucky Stone

Red Coral (Moonga) is the recommended lucky stone for Mesham. Wearing it can help enhance their courage and physical vitality while balancing their fiery energy.
